3. Heuristic Methods

The NP-Hardness of the problem might be seen as an insurmountable problem. How can i find a solution in a feasible amount of time since the estimated time to solve it is exponential? This question is the initial point to explore the heuristic methods. Heuristic is a problem-solving paradigms whose main characteristic is the trade-off between optimal and speed. In fact, the goal of heuristic methods is not to provide the optimal solution to the problem, but to obtain a good feasible solution in a reasonable execution time.

Nearest Neighbours

The nearest neighbours method is one of the most simple working algorithm to provide a solution to TSP. The generic approach of this algorithm is called greedy, a framework to produce algorithms, that suggests to make the current best choice without think the consequences of that action.
